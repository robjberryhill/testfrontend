Silence & Solidtude

December 1, 2018

Practical Tips:
1. Find a queit place alone and start 10 minutes of silence with God.
2. Come with a phrase that will help you keep focused. "I've come to meet You in this place"
3. Come with a question for Jesus or say what is truly on your heart.
This doesnt mean you have to leave with an answer, but it's learning to leave it as His feet.
4. REST, nap...Rest is holy.
